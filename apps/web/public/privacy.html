Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Orbital Traffic is a satellite and near-Earth-object tracker. This page explains what data the app uses, what it doesn't, and where anything that does get sent leaves your device.

Location

The app requests your device location only if you turn on ISS Pass Alerts in the iOS/Android app (this feature is not present in the web version). Your coordinates are sent to our server each time pass alerts are refreshed (this happens automatically each time the app opens, if alerts are enabled), to calculate upcoming ISS passes for your location, and are not stored beyond that calculation. Pass alerts themselves are scheduled as local notifications directly on your device — we do not know when, or whether, a notification is shown to you.

Fonts

The app loads two fonts directly from Google Fonts. As with any third-party font host, Google receives the IP address of each visitor's device as part of delivering those files. We do not control or receive this data ourselves.

What we don't collect

  • No accounts, no sign-in, no personal profile of any kind
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising, and no ad SDKs of any kind
  • No data is sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes

Third-party services this app talks to

These are services the app fetches public orbital/space data from. None of them receive any information that identifies you:

  • CelesTrak — public-domain satellite orbital element data
  • Open Notify — current ISS/Tiangong crew roster
  • NASA — public ISS activity information

The app's backend runs on Cloudflare, which, like any web infrastructure provider, processes standard connection metadata (such as IP address) as a normal part of serving requests. Orbital Traffic itself does not read, log, or use this information.

Data shown in the app

Orbital positions are computed on your own device from cached and periodically refreshed public orbital element data. This data is accurate as of each refresh, and is provided for informational purposes only, not for navigation or operational use.

Changes to this policy

If what the app collects changes, this page will be updated and the "last updated" date above will change accordingly.
